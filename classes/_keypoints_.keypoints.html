    • Defined in keypoints.tsx:39

    Constructor for Openpose output.

    Pass in outputs from Openpose (in the order given to you). Outputs are arrays of tuples -- X, Y, score. Draw any points where the score is greater than 0.

    If face_pose, hand_left_pose, or hand_right_pose are included, include those too.

    If show_left_right is true, color the left side of the body red and the right side blue (except for face, which will always be white).
